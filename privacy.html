Plain summary: Behold respects your privacy. We collect the minimum information needed to run the app, never sell it, and use it only to provide Bible study features. This page explains exactly what we collect, why, and how to remove it.

1. Who We Are

Behold Scripture ("Behold," "we," "our," or "us") is an iOS and Android Bible study app developed and operated by Lamar Adams, an independent developer based in Pennsylvania, United States.

The App provides Bible reading, notes, highlights, study tools, AI-powered conversations, and related features.

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly

  • Account information. Email address and authentication credentials. Passwords are securely hashed by our authentication provider; we do not view or store plaintext passwords.
  • Bible study content. Notes, highlights, reading progress, saved verses, bookmarks, and AI conversation history.
  • AI conversations. Questions you submit, messages exchanged with the AI, and relevant contextual information (such as the passage you're studying and notes you've created) needed to generate responses.
  • Support and feedback. Email communications, support requests, bug reports, and feedback you send to us voluntarily.

Information collected automatically

  • Device and app information. Device type, operating system version, app version, language settings.
  • Crash reports and diagnostics. Anonymized error logs and performance information used to fix bugs and improve reliability.

Information we do not collect

  • We do not track your precise location.
  • We do not access your contacts, photos, or media libraries.
  • We do not access your microphone.
  • We do not use advertising trackers for cross-context behavioral advertising.
  • We do not sell personal data.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Operate and maintain the App
  • Provide Bible reading, notes, highlights, and AI features
  • Sync content across your devices
  • Personalize AI responses with your own notes (never anyone else's)
  • Improve reliability and fix bugs
  • Respond to support and feedback requests
  • Detect abuse, fraud, or security threats
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. AI Features

Behold includes AI-powered features ("Ask Behold") that generate responses based on user prompts and related context.

To provide these features, your messages and related context are processed by Anthropic, the provider of Claude. Anthropic processes this content according to their own Privacy Policy. Per Anthropic's policies, content submitted via the Claude API is not used to train their public AI models.

We may add additional AI providers in the future. If we do, this policy will be updated and any new providers will be disclosed here.

Important AI disclaimer: AI-generated responses are for informational and educational purposes only. They may contain inaccuracies, be incomplete, or fail to reflect specific theological traditions. AI responses are not a substitute for professional spiritual, medical, legal, or mental health guidance. For full disclosure see our AI Use Disclosure.

5. Third-Party Services

To operate Behold, we rely on a small set of trusted third-party providers:

  • Anthropic — AI conversation processing (Claude)
  • Supabase — Account and content storage, when account features are enabled

If we add or change service providers in the future, this policy will be updated.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only:

  • With the trusted service providers listed above, to operate the App
  • When required by law, legal process, or valid government request
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of Behold, our users, or others
  • To investigate fraud, abuse, or security issues
  • In connection with a merger, acquisition, or business transfer (you would be notified)

7. Data Retention

We keep your content (notes, highlights, conversations) for as long as your account remains active or as needed to provide the App. You can delete individual items at any time within the App.

If you request account deletion, we will remove your associated personal content within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

8. Security

We use commercially reasonable safeguards including encryption in transit (HTTPS), access controls, and secure authentication. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Your Privacy Rights

Access, correction, and deletion

  • View, edit, and delete your notes and highlights at any time inside the App
  • Delete your account, which removes your associated personal content
  • Email us to request a copy of your data, correct inaccuracies, or request deletion

California, Virginia, Colorado, Connecticut, Utah residents (and others)

If you are a resident of a U.S. state with comprehensive privacy laws (CCPA/CPRA, VCDPA, CPA, CTDPA, UCPA), you have additional rights including the right to know what personal information we collect, the right to request deletion, and the right to opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information. We do not sell or share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.

European Economic Area, United Kingdom, and Switzerland residents

If you are located in the EEA, UK, or Switzerland, you have rights under the GDPR including access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, and objection. The legal basis for our processing is generally your consent (which you can withdraw at any time) or our legitimate interest in providing the App.

To exercise any of these rights, email AdamsDev.Labs@gmail.com.

10. Children's Privacy

Behold is intended for users ages 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us at AdamsDev.Labs@gmail.com and we will delete it promptly.

11. International Data Transfers

Behold is operated from the United States. If you use the App from outside the United States, your information will be transferred to, processed, and stored in the United States and other jurisdictions where our service providers operate. By using the App, you consent to such transfers.

12. Bible Translation Sources

The Bible translations included in Behold are either in the public domain or available under permissive free-use licenses:

  • Behold Standard English (BSE), based on the World English Bible — public domain
  • King James Version (KJV) — public domain
  • Berean Standard Bible (BSB) — free for any use including commercial

Translation attribution is also available in the App's About screen.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the "Effective" date at the top and, where appropriate, notify you within the App. Continued use of Behold after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
